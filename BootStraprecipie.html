Just add water pancakes

Noah Perkins

The is a breakfast item that is quick and easy to make. Make this when you're strapped for time but not that strapped. If you're super late just grab a granola bar and leave your home immediately

  • Pancake mix
  • Water
  • butter
  • Bowl
  • spoon
  • spatula
  • skillet
Directions
  1. Pour pancake mix into bowl
  2. Add the directed amount of water
  3. Stir mixture until smooth
  4. Pour mixture into hot skillet
  5. Cook on one side for 2 minutes then flip for another two minutes
  6. Place on plate, buter pancakes
  7. serve
